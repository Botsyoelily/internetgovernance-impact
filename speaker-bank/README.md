## ‚úçÔ∏è Join the Bank

To add yourself or nominate someone else:

1. **Click the pencil icon** (Edit this file) at the top right
2. **Scroll down** to the "Speakers" section
3. **Copy the template below** and add your details:

```markdown
### **[Your Full Name]** | [Country]
- **Topics of Expertise:** [Topic 1, Topic 2, Topic 3]
- **Languages:** [Language 1, Language 2]
- **Available For:** [Speaking, Panels, Moderation, Training, Mentorship, etc.]
- **Link:** [LinkedIn/Website/Profile URL]
```

4. **Paste it** in alphabetical order by first name
5. **Fill in your information**
6. **Scroll down** and click "Commit changes"

**Tips:**
- Keep your topics clear and specific (3-5 topics is ideal)
- Include your LinkedIn, personal website, or any professional profile link
- Be honest about your availability - it helps organizers match you with the right opportunities
